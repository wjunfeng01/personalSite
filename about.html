02 : About me

Store franchise owner

At age 20, I was fortunate to be given a chance to start my first Bubble Tea franchise outlet under a renowned local brand. In one year, this store has been bought over due to a high turnover rate and a healthy balance sheet, providing me with the liquidity to explore other ventures.

Co-founder of PegasusCarModz

Automobiles have always been my passion. Therefore, I started this service to help like minded individuals looking to give their cars a sportier look by modifying their bodykits.

Full Stack Web Developer

I picked up web development as I aspire to inculcate technology to solve problems in the world. I'm proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript, DOM, jQuery, NodeJS, ExpressJS and API integrations. My other programming languages are C, C#, C++, Python, Web scraping, and Computer vision.

Education

At Ngee Ann Polytechnic Engineering Science, we were trained in the field of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, with a heavy emphasis on Programming and Research. I graduated with a GPA of 3.84/4.0 with the NP Merit Award. Projects I completed were a Computer Vision Program for Hewlett Packard, Hardware Verifications for DSTA, and 3D Printing for NP. I was also Vice-President of Engineering Science Society, a member of PAP Grassroot, a member of Leo Club, Sandbox, and Toastmasters.
